60 Minute Timer FAQ

How do I start a 60 minute timer?
Click the "Start 60 Minutes" button on this page. The timer is pre-set to sixty minutes and begins counting down immediately. An alarm will sound when the 60 minutes are complete.
How many seconds are in 60 minutes?
60 minutes contains exactly 3,600 seconds (60 minutes x 60 seconds per minute = 3,600 seconds). Our timer displays both minutes and seconds as it counts down.
Can I pause the 60 minute timer?
Yes! Click the Pause button or press the Space key to pause the countdown at any time. Click Start or press Space again to resume from where you left off. Use Reset to restart the full 60 minutes.
