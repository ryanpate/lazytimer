Cooking Timer FAQ

How long should I cook soft boiled eggs?
For perfect soft boiled eggs with runny yolks, cook for 6-7 minutes in boiling water. Place eggs in boiling water, set your timer for 6 minutes for very soft yolks or 7 minutes for slightly firmer yolks, then immediately transfer to an ice bath to stop cooking.
How long does pasta take to cook?
Most dried pasta takes 8-12 minutes to cook al dente. Spaghetti typically needs 9-10 minutes, penne 11-12 minutes, and angel hair only 4-5 minutes. Always check the package directions and taste test 1-2 minutes before the suggested time.
How long to cook rice?
White rice typically takes 15-18 minutes after reaching a boil, while brown rice needs 40-45 minutes. For white rice, bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-18 minutes. Let it rest for 5 minutes off heat before fluffing.
What is the best kitchen timer?
The best kitchen timer is one that's always accessible, easy to set, and provides clear alerts. Online cooking timers work on any device you already have, require no batteries, and are more convenient than physical kitchen timers.
How do I time multiple dishes cooking at once?
For multiple dishes, write down when each item needs to go in and come out. Start with the longest-cooking item first, then add others based on their cook times. You can open multiple browser tabs to run separate timers simultaneously.
How long do I bake chicken breast at 400¬∞F?
Boneless chicken breasts take 20-25 minutes at 400¬∞F (200¬∞C). Thicker breasts need 25-30 minutes. Always use a meat thermometer to verify the internal temperature reaches 165¬∞F (74¬∞C) for food safety. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes after cooking for juicier results.
How long to cook a medium steak?
For a 1-inch thick steak, cook 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness (145¬∞F internal temperature). For medium-rare, cook 3-4 minutes per side. Let the steak rest for 5 minutes after cooking to allow juices to redistribute throughout the meat.
How long do cookies take to bake?
Most cookies bake in 8-12 minutes at 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Thin, crispy cookies need 10-12 minutes, while soft, chewy cookies are done in 8-10 minutes. Remove cookies when edges are golden but centers look slightly underdone‚Äîthey'll continue cooking on the hot pan.
How long to boil potatoes for mashed potatoes?
Cubed potatoes (1-inch pieces) take 15-20 minutes to boil until fork-tender. Whole small potatoes need 20-25 minutes. Start timing after the water returns to a boil. Potatoes are ready when a fork easily slides through with no resistance.
How long does it take to preheat an oven?
Most home ovens take 10-15 minutes to preheat to 350¬∞F. Higher temperatures like 425-450¬∞F may take 15-20 minutes. Electric ovens typically preheat faster than gas ovens. Wait for the preheat indicator or audible signal before adding food.
