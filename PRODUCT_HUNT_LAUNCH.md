### Timing
- **Launch at 12:01 AM PST** (Product Hunt resets daily at midnight PST)
- This gives you a full 24 hours to accumulate votes

## Responding to Comments

### Be Present
- Check Product Hunt every 30-60 minutes on launch day
- Respond to EVERY comment within 1 hour
- Be genuine, helpful, and grateful

### Common Questions & Answers

**"How is this different from [competitor]?"**
```
Great question! Unlike [competitor], LazyTimer:
- Requires no signup or account
- Has 15 specialized pages for different use cases
- Is completely free with no premium tier
- Focuses on beautiful, distraction-free design

We're not trying to be everything - just the best free timer experience.
```

**"Will you add [feature]?"**
```
Love this idea! I've added it to my list.

Right now I'm focused on [current priority], but [feature]
is definitely something I want to explore.

Thanks for the suggestion!
```

**"Nice design!"**
```
Thank you so much! I spent a lot of time on the visual
experience - wanted it to feel premium even though it's free.

Really appreciate you checking it out!
```
