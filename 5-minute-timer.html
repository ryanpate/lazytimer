What Can You Do in 5 Minutes?

Five minutes is the perfect duration for many quick activities. It's long enough to be productive but short enough to maintain focus. Here are popular uses for a 5 minute timer:

  • Quick meditation or breathing exercises - A 5 minute mindfulness session can reduce stress and improve focus
  • Work breaks - The Pomodoro Technique recommends 5 minute breaks between work sessions
  • Soft-boiled eggs - Perfect timing for eggs with runny yolks
  • Plank exercise - A challenging but achievable fitness goal
  • Quick cleanup - Set a timer and see how much you can tidy in 5 minutes
  • Brewing tea - Many teas steep perfectly in 5 minutes
  • Phone breaks - Limit social media scrolling to just 5 minutes
